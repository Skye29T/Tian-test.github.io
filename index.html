My favorite national park, Yellowstone National Park

Yellowstone National Park is the first national park in the United States. It is mainly located in Wyoming, and partly in Montana and Idaho. It was established on March 1, 1872 after US President Grant signed a bill passed by Congress. It is the first national park in the world.
